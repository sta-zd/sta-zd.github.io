Travel to Manchester!

Manchester is the place to go...

From the best shopping experience, to eye-opening national parks, nightclubs to museums, Manchester has it all.

The BEST places to go in Manchester:

  • Trafford Centre...

    • A beautiful, marble-structured shopping centre full of deliciously endulged bars and restaurants.

  • The Arndale...

    • Not just any shopping centre, but the heart of Manchester City Centre, where most Mancunians refer to it as 'town'.

  • Manchester Museum...

    • A wonderful museum equipted with vast collections ranging from Ancient Egypt, Animals to Dinosaur fossils! Also located next to Manchester University.

  • The Museum of Science and Industry MOSI...


    • Located in the city centre, the MOSI is a fascinating museum with a variety of interactive and educating features that kids and adults can enjoy.

  • Heaton Park...


    • An enormous and well-reserved yet popular park that is not far from the city centre, this park has a small farm with animals that you can feed and ride too.

  • Lyme Park...


    • Slightly further of from the city centre, but worth the travel. One of many national trust parks in the UK. It has stunning scenery which is perfect for taking photos.

    .


The list is endless, there are so many things to do in Manchester, but I have listed only SOME of the best spots to visit!

We Mancunians are very proud of our diverse, lively and cultured city and love nothing more to brag about it!


Enjoy your visit!
